    What Are Cryptocurrencies?
    Cryptocurrencies are digital, decentralized currencies that were created to work as a medium of exchange. The cryptocurrency market offers traders a new way of investing; free from intermediaries such as governments or rating agencies. Hence, price movements on cryptos are driven by a multitude of factors such as market demand and relative news.

    How does Crypto CFD trading work?
